FAQ

Frequently asked questions

How does the personality quiz work? +
The quiz has 8 questions covering your travel pace, accommodation preferences, activity interests, social style, and more. Your answers are scored across 6 personality types, and the dominant type becomes your travel profile. The whole thing takes under 2 minutes.
Can I retake the quiz? +
Yes! You can retake the quiz as many times as you like. Your travel personality can evolve — and different trips might call for a different side of you. Retake it from your dashboard anytime.
How are itineraries generated? +
Our AI combines your personality type, chosen destination, and activity preferences to build a day-by-day schedule. Each time of day (morning, afternoon, evening) is assigned activities that fit your energy level, interests, and travel style — including hidden gems most travelers miss.
Is Itinerate free to use? +
Yes — signing up, taking the quiz, getting destination recommendations, and generating your first itinerary are all completely free. We believe everyone deserves a trip that truly fits them.
